User Guide

Create a Deck with AI

Create a reusable CSV deck in three quick steps, then upload it to start studying. Flashcard fields are required in every row; multiple-choice fields are optional per row.

  1. Open the AI Prompt

    Use the built-in prompt template to guide your AI assistant.

  2. Generate CSV output

    Paste the prompt into your AI assistant and replace the placeholder in the Source(s) section with your learning material. Use the full 8-column header, keep flashcard fields filled in every row, and include MC fields only when needed.

  3. Create and save a CSV file

    Paste the generated CSV below, choose a filename, and save it on your device.
